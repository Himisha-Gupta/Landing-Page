Ivy Hospital Mohali is the First Flagship Hospital of Ivy Healthcare Group. It is a 180-bedded multi Super Speciality Tertiary Care Hospital. The hospital started its operations way back in 2008 and is the leading hospital in the region. It is a state-of-the-art modern facility accredited by NABH situated in a prominent location at Mohali. The hospital is providing tertiary level medical care in Cardiology, Cardio Vascular & Thoracic Surgery, Oncology, Neurosurgery, Neurology, Urology, Nephrology, Renal Transplant Surgery, Laparoscopic surgery, Vascular Surgery, Orthopedics, Plastic Reconstruction & Cosmetic Surgery besides other departments.
